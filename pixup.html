PixUp is a peer-to-peer delivery service that is currently under development. PixUp is a platform to connect vehicle owners (drivers) with residents/businesses that potentially need delivery service (users). Drivers pick up requests made by users and offer instant delivery from and/or to the users, who in turn offer monetary compensation to the drivers.

Interview and Persona

The personae are generated from a series of interviews. A total of six potential customers (three drivers and three users) were interviewed. A thorough interview protocol that inquired about online TV streaming experience was prepared in advance by us and incorporated during the interview process. From the information we collected, we generated three personae based on result of interviews, which reflected one user and two drivers of different age range.

Storyboard

We used Bitstrips to create our storyboard after creating the persona and having shown it to the client.

Comparative Analysis

We compared PixUp with several competitors that offer express and peer-to-peer delivery services. A total of 7 competitors (Major competitors: Kanga, Dolly, and UberRush; Minor Competitors: Amazon Fresh, Google Express, UPS, and UHaul) were chosen and evaluated under several different categories. We analyzed and discussed our data and research to create a table that compares PixUp to other competitors.
